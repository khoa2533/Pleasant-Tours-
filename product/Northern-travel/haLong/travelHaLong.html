Ha Long is known as a tourist city, a major tourist center of Vietnam. In 2015, the number of tourists to Ha Long reached over 6 million. Ha Long Bay has been recognized by UNESCO as a World Natural Heritage twice. In addition, at 2 o'clock on November 12, 2012 (Vietnam time), Ha Long Bay was declared by New7Wonders as one of the new seven natural wonders of the world. Ha Long has received the National Cup on the environment as a green - clean - beautiful city.

Attached to Ha Long Bay, the wards of Bai Chay, Tuan Chau, Hung Thang are developing hotels and restaurants and building tourist projects. Ha Long has hundreds of large and small hotels with many 4, 5 star hotels, fully equipped and modern, meeting the needs of international and domestic guests.

Ha Long and Tuan Chau beaches are increasingly being renovated and developed. On the west bank of the city (Hon Gai) is planning to build 2 artificial beaches to serve the city's residents.

Sun Group has built the largest Sun World Ha Long Park in Vietnam at Bai Chay and Hon Gai. There are also many high-class tourism items of large domestic and foreign corporations such as Vin Group, FLC, ...

In addition to the bay, with nearly 30 caves have been discovered, Thien Cung, Hon Dua, Dau Go caves, Sung Sot cave have been put into tourism service for many years.

The cluster of historical sites and scenic spots in the city center includes Bai Tho Mountain, the Temple of Dong Hai Dai Vuong Tran Quoc Nghien, and Long Tien Pagoda on the side of Bai Tho Mountain. Historical sites, cultural activities and habitats, pine hills, city parks are being exploited to welcome millions of visitors in the coming years.
