Tay Ninh is a province in the Southeast region of Vietnam.

Tay Ninh is located at the bridge between Ho Chi Minh City and the capital Phnom Penh, Kingdom of Cambodia and is one of the provinces located in the Southern Key Economic Zone. The province has Tay Ninh city, located 99 km from Ho Chi Minh City along National Highway 22, 40 km from the Cambodian border to the northwest.

The land of Tay Ninh from ancient times was a land belonging to Thuy Chan Lap, with the name Romdum Ray, meaning "Elephant Cage" because this place only has dense forests with all kinds of wild animals such as tigers, elephants, leopards, snakes. ,... Live. The aboriginal people here lived in extreme poverty, until the Vietnamese came to reclaim this land.
